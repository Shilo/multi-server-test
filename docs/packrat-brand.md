## Mascot

PackRat is a cute rat-package mascot: rounded mouse/rat ears, whiskers, and a
box-shaped face/body. It should feel friendly, lightweight, and practical.

## Icon

- Source: `icon.svg`
- Format: transparent SVG
- Style: two-tone, cartoon, readable on light and dark backgrounds
- Do not add a background plate or extra highlight colors unless the whole
  palette is revisited.

## Color Scheme

| Role | Hex | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Fill | `#F6C98D` | Warm mouse/package tan. |
| Outline/details | `#8A5729` | Medium cocoa chosen for light/dark readability. |

The icon intentionally uses only these two colors.
